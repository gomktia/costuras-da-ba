# Google Business Profile Optimization

Guide for setting up and optimizing a Google Business Profile for local service businesses.

## Profile Setup

1. Create profile at [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Choose the most specific category available:
   - Primary: "Tailor" or "Costureira" or "Alfaiataria"
   - If not available: "Clothing Alteration Service" or "Sewing Shop"
   - Additional categories: "Dressmaker", "Bridal Shop" (if applicable)
3. Add complete business information matching NAP on website exactly
4. Verify by postcard, phone, or email (Google will present available options)

## Profile Optimization

### Business Information
- **Name**: Exact business name (no keyword stuffing)
- **Address**: Full Brazilian format with CEP and bairro
- **Phone**: Same number as website, with +55 country code
- **Website**: Link to the landing page
- **Hours**: Keep updated, including holiday hours
- **Description**: Up to 750 characters. Include primary keywords naturally. Mention services, location, and experience.

### Categories
- Set the most specific primary category
- Add up to 9 additional categories for related services
- Review categories periodically ‚Äî Google adds new ones

### Services
Add each service with description and optional price range:
- Service name: "Ajustes e Reparos"
- Description: Brief, keyword-rich
- Price: "A partir de R$ XX" or price range

### Posts
Post regularly (weekly is ideal):

| Post Type | Content | Frequency |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| Update | Tips, behind-the-scenes, new service | Weekly |
| Offer | Seasonal promotion, first-visit discount | Monthly |

Post tips:
- Include a photo or video
- Add a CTA button (Call, Learn More, Book)
- Keep text under 300 words
- Include relevant keywords naturally

## Monitoring

Check Google Business Profile Insights monthly:
- Search queries that triggered the listing
- Photo views compared to competitors
- Direction requests and calls
- Website clicks
- Review trends
